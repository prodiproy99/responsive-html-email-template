PRE-CONFERENCE CLASS:

Standardizing Transmission P&C Drawings
and Relay Settings to Comply with PRC-027-1

Monday, March 28
1:00pm-3:00pm
In-person seminar

In this free seminar, we will examine the requirements for PRC-027-1 and evaluate available best practices for accomplishing these equirements most effectively. We will then provide practical steps for establishing a utility-wide standardized development process for protection and control drawings and relay settings, empowering utilities to maintain consistency, increase reliability, and reduce errors.

We will also introduce an automation tool which streamlines these processes simplifying standardization and compliance.
